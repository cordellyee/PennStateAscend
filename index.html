Ascend at Penn State

Connect. Inspire. Ascend.

Ascend is a nonprofit organization dedicated to promoting and enhancing the presence and influence of Asian professionals in business settings. With student and professional chapters spanning across the United States and Canada, Ascend has become the largest non-profit organization to serve the Pan-Asian demographic.

Goals

Enable

Enable young professionals and emerging leaders to pursue their dreams and achieve new professional heights. We prepare our members by hosting workshops on soft and hard skills.

Connect

Connect Pan-Asian leaders across industries through a network of seasoned professionals and emerging leaders. We set up opportunities to expand networks and opportunities.

Grow

Grow our members into high-caliber leaders by providing meaningful leadership opportunities. Assist our partners to achieve business growth through world-class diversity and inclusion practices.

  • Why Join Ascend?

    As an Ascend or AscendNAAMBA member, you have access to outstanding leadership and professional development programs, unique Pan-Asian executive networks, and opportunities to develop your leadership skills and influence in the community. Membership also provides you with exclusive access to our members-only benefits and discounts and access to Ascend and AscendNAAMBA events both at the national and local chapter level.

    Why Join Ascend?

  • Benefits of Ascend Membership

    Opportunity to connect and engage with mentors and senior executives across industries

    Participate in career development and career coaching workshops

    Access to invaluable career advancement advice and opportunities

Mission: Be the premier professional organization dedicated to enabling its members, corporate partners and the professional Pan-Asian community to leverage the leadership and global business potential of Pan-Asian professionals worldwide.
